## Overview

Household Expense is a personal and household finance tracker. Your expense, income, and budget data are stored **primarily on your device**. We do not operate a cloud account service for your financial records unless you optionally enable a feedback sync API (disabled by default).

## Information we process

### Stored on your device
- Account profile (name, household name, email, phone — used for local identity recovery)
- PIN or password (stored as a one-way hash, not plain text)
- Expenses, income, budgets, categories, bank import files you choose to import
- Encrypted local database (SQLCipher) and secure storage for credentials

### Optional / third-party
- **Google AdMob** (free trial): may collect advertising ID and usage data per [Google’s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). Ads are not shown after premium purchase.
- **Google Play Billing**: purchase status for subscription; processed by Google.
- **Email / phone links**: if you tap support phone or email, your device’s dialer or mail app opens — we do not receive that action unless you send a message.
- **Feedback** (if you use Send feedback): message content and optional device info you submit.

## How we use information

- Provide expense tracking, budgets, reports, and exports
- Authenticate you on the device (PIN, password, biometrics)
- Show ads during the free trial period
- Process in-app purchases through Google Play

We do **not** sell your personal financial data.

## Data sharing

We do not share your on-device financial records with third parties. Third-party SDKs (AdMob, Google Play) may process data according to their own policies when those features are used.

## Security

- Local database encryption
- Android backup disabled for app data
- Release builds use code shrinking (R8)

## Your choices

- Export or delete data via in-app backup/restore and “Clear all data”
- Uninstall the app to remove local data
- Purchase premium to remove ads

## Children

This app is not directed at children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change when we do.
